What is Global Goal 15?


Global Goal 15 is one of the 17 Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs) established by the United Nations in 2015 as part of the 2030 Agenda for Sustainable Development. Global Goal 15 focuses on "Life on Land" and aims to protect, restore, and promote sustainable use of terrestrial ecosystems, manage forests sustainably, combat desertification, halt and reverse land degradation, and halt biodiversity loss.

MISSION


" Our mission is to promote awareness and appreciation of
life on land by providing accessible and engaging educational resources,
inspiring individuals to take action towards conservation and sustainable practices,
and fostering a global community dedicated to preserving terrestrial ecosystems for future generations "


TARGETS

CONSERVE AND RESTORE TERRESTRIAL AND FRESHWATER ECOSYSTEMS

By 2020, ensure the conservation, restoration and sustainable use of terrestrial and inland freshwater ecosystems and their services, in particular forests, wetlands, mountains and drylands, in line with obligations under international agreements.

END DEFORESTATION AND RESTORE DEGRADED FORESTS

By 2020, promote the implementation of sustainable management of all types of forests, halt deforestation, restore degraded forests and substantially increase afforestation and reforestation globally.

END DESERTIFICATION AND RESTORE DEGRADED LAND

By 2030, combat desertification, restore degraded land and soil, including land affected by desertification, drought and floods, and strive to achieve a land degradation-neutral world.



Thing to do

1. Find a Goal 15 charity you want to support. Any donation, big or small, can make a difference

2. Reduce your use of paper. Avoid printing and substitute it with electronic devices or carriers.

3. Don’t shop for pets! Visit your local animal shelter and adopt an animal there.

4. Don’t buy products tested on animals: Check the labels on the products you buy.

5. Recycle paper, glass, plastic, metal and old electronics.

6. Compost. Composting food scraps can reduce climate impact while also recycling nutrients.

7. Don’t use pesticides. To protect and sustain the quality of soil, don’t use chemicals while growing plants.
